THE KRUSTY KRAB

Home of the world-famous Krabby Patty

At the Krusty Krab, customers can indulge in the famous Krabby Patty burger, made from a secret recipe that only the restaurant's owner knows.

Other popular menu items include the Krusty Krab Pizza, Kelp Fries, and the Chum Bucket Supreme, which is the Krusty Krab's rival burger from the competing Chum Bucket restaurant.

The restaurant's interior features marine-inspired decor, complete with ship wheels, nets, and other ocean-themed elements.

The staff are friendly and welcoming, eager to make each customer's experience a memorable one.
